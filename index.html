Why Vendorsync?

  • Centralized stock data management with real-time Firestore syncing.
  • Automatic stock initialization from delivered orders.
  • Manual and bulk stock updates via intuitive forms or CSV uploads.
  • Smart threshold monitoring and low-stock alerts.
  • Predictive analytics for suggested orders and demand forecasting.
  • Vendor review and confirmation before automated order placement.
  • Visual reports for stock status, delivery history, and sales insights.
  • Sales prediction engine to help optimize inventory planning.
