About Vendorsync

Vendorsync is a Vendor-to-Supplier Management System designed to bridge the communication gap between vendors and suppliers, especially in industries supermarkets. Built with a modern technology stack, Vendorsync enables real-time visibility, accountability, and seamless collaboration in the supply chain.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify supply chain communication by providing smart, automated tools that reduce shortages, improve delivery accuracy, and build stronger vendor-supplier relationships.

Who is it for?

VendorSync is designed for:

  • Retail store managers and supermarket operators
  • Vendors managing multiple product lines
  • Suppliers handling recurring deliveries
  • Businesses looking to automate stock and order systems

Why Choose Vendorsync?

  • Smart stock threshold alerts to avoid shortages
  • Real-time inventory sync between vendors and suppliers
  • Insightful analytics to track trends and cut costs
  • Easy onboarding and management of supplier accounts
  • Paperless workflows for order approvals and communication
